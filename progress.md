Original prompt: create a modernized clone of the arcade game Discs of Tron. This should be a Virtual Reality webxr game that supports VR mode, AR mode, and normal mode. We can call it Digi World. It should be 3D like many of the popular 3D games that are out there with humanistic like enemies, and a style very alike to the modern Tron movies. It should be an updated spin on the classic game. It should also include a secondary game modeling the light bikes where the player rides his bike and tries to destroy enemies with the light wall that comes from the bike, as they try the same to the player. The game is first person where the player is in the game and throwing/dodging discs and jumping between circular platforms or driving the bike. In AR mode, the game uses pre-set room setups and brings the enemies into the space where discs systematically break apart the space and revealing the digital world in the breaks. Award winning graphics and designs and emmersive experience.
